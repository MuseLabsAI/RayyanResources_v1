About Rayyan Resources

Rayyan Resources is a Canadian-investment mineral exploration services company operating across Saudi Arabia, Oman, UAE, Sudan, United Kingdom, and Canada.

We specialize in delivering innovative, efficient, and technology-driven exploration solutions aimed at discovering and unlocking the value of mineral resources. With a focus on global standards and sustainability, we provide world-class services tailored to meet the needs of the mining industry.
